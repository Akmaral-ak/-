buns

A small bun, sometimes sweet, bread or roll. While they come in a variety of shapes and sizes, they are most often hand-sized or smaller, with a round top and a flat bottom. Buns are usually made with flour, sugar, milk, yeast, and butter.

bakery products

Baking is a generic name for baked goods and confectionery products made by the baking method, as well as the process itself. Confectionery products are made from other types of dough, mainly with the addition of fats and sugar. The classic types of pastry are biscuits, pies and cakes.
